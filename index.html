About us

Pure Vanilla, Done Right

Refined Vanilla is a Fabric-powered anarchy Minecraft server, exactly as it was meant to be played - free from the game-altering bugs and compromises introduced by Paper and Folia.

Running on Minecraft 1.21.10, with full support for both Java and Bedrock Edition, the server delivers a truly authentic vanilla experience.

With faithful mechanics ideal for PvP and redstone, Refined Vanilla offers a competitive environment where true vanilla mastery thrives.
